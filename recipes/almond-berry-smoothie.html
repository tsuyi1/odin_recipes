Almond Berry Smoothie

Decription

These Blueberry Almond Oatmeal Smoothies make a quick, filling, and satisfying breakfast. They’re full of fruit, fiber, and packed with protein so they’ll keep you full for hours.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup frozen blueberries
  • 1 banana
  • ½ cup almond milk
  • 1 tablespoon almond butter
  • Water as needed

Steps

  1. Combine blueberries, banana, almond milk, and almond butter in a blender; blend until smooth, adding water for a thinner smoothie.
